What is TENS?

TENS or Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation,
offers drug-free relief by utilizing electrical pulses that disrupt pain signals,
providing comfort during menstruation.

How does My Circe work?

The Circe period pain device sends gentle electrical pulses through electrode pads placed on the skin,
distracting your brain from processing period pain signals.

Attach the battery pack to the pad.

Place the adhesive pads on your skin.

Choose your intensity level and experience instant drug-free relief from menstrual cramps!
